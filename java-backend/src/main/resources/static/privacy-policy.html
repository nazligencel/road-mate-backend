RoadMate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how RoadMate collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile photo, and password when you create an account.
  • Location Data: Your real-time location to show nearby travelers, enable SOS features, and display your position on the map. Location is collected only while the app is in use and with your permission.
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle type, brand, model, and year that you voluntarily provide for your profile.
  • Messages: Content of messages you send to other users through our in-app messaging system.
  • Photos: Profile photos, gallery photos, and vehicle photos you upload.
  • Device Information: Device type, push notification tokens for delivering notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the RoadMate service
  • Show your location to nearby travelers on the map
  • Enable communication between users through messaging
  • Send push notifications for SOS alerts, messages, and meeting requests
  • Provide the community discussion features
  • Process subscription purchases and manage your account
  • Improve and optimize our services

3. Location Data

RoadMate uses your location to connect you with nearby travelers. Your location data is:

  • Collected only when you actively use the app and grant permission
  • Shared with other users in your vicinity as part of the core functionality
  • Used for SOS emergency alerts to notify nearby users
  • Not sold to third parties for advertising purposes

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Other Users: Your profile information, location, and public posts are visible to other RoadMate users as part of the service functionality.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services for hosting (Railway), database (Neon PostgreSQL), push notifications (Expo), and subscription management (RevenueCat).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing
  • API communications are secured with JWT authentication
  • Database connections use SSL encryption

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Block other users to prevent interaction
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our systems. Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period.

8. Children's Privacy

RoadMate is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In (Authentication)
  • Google Places API (Location search)
  • Expo (Push notifications)
  • RevenueCat (Subscription management)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of RoadMate after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
